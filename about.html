About Mindrink

Mindrink is an independent project built with care. It's about awareness, balance, and helping you make mindful choices around drinking—without judgment, without pressure, just clarity.

Privacy

Your Data Stays Local

All your drinking data, reflections, and goals are stored locally on your device. We don't sync anything to the cloud automatically. Your privacy is important to us.

Future Analytics

We may add optional analytics in the future to help improve the app, but we will never include your drinking history or personal reflections. Ever.

You're in Control

You can delete your data at any time (this feature is coming soon). Uninstalling the app will also clear all your local data. You own your information.

As we continue to build and improve Mindrink, we may update our privacy practices. We'll always keep you informed and maintain the same commitment to your privacy.

Terms & Disclaimer

Not Medical Advice

Mindrink is a tool for awareness and tracking. It is not medical advice, and it doesn't replace professional guidance. If you have concerns about your drinking, please consult a healthcare provider.

Drinking Limits

The limits and guidelines shown in the app are based on public health recommendations, simplified for clarity. These are general guidelines, not personal prescriptions. Everyone's body and situation are different.

Your Responsibility

You are responsible for your own drinking decisions. Mindrink helps you track and reflect, but the choices you make are yours. Use the app as a tool for awareness, not as a substitute for your own judgment.
