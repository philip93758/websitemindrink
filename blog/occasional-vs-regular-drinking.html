Occasional heavy drinking vs regular drinking: what's the difference?

Looks at different drinking patterns and why they feel different, without labeling them as good or bad.

Occasional heavy drinking and regular drinking often trigger different reactions — socially and emotionally.

Why these patterns feel different

Occasional heavy drinking is usually situational: events, celebrations, social momentum. Regular drinking tends to integrate more quietly into routine.

Why neither label is enough

Neither pattern is inherently good or bad. They simply interact differently with energy, sleep, mood, and recovery.

Understanding the difference helps move away from labels and toward observation.
