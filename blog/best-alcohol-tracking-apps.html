Best alcohol tracking apps: what to look for (and why simplicity matters)

Compares different alcohol tracking approaches and explains what actually helps over time.

Alcohol tracking apps vary widely. Some focus on goals, others on streaks, others on strict limits.

What actually helps long-term

The most helpful tools tend to share a few traits:

  • low friction
  • neutral language
  • pattern-focused views
  • flexibility

Where complexity backfires

Apps that overload users with rules or notifications often fail long-term. Simplicity matters because tracking is something people do alongside life, not instead of it.

Mindrink is one example of an app designed around observation rather than correction, but different tools suit different needs.
