Why weekly drinking patterns matter more than single drinking days

Explains why looking at patterns over time gives more insight than focusing on individual days.

It's easy to focus on individual drinking occasions: a party, a celebration, a stressful evening. But isolated days rarely tell the full story.

Patterns vs events

What matters more is pattern — how often drinking occurs, how it's distributed, and whether it's habitual or situational.

How accumulation works

Weekly views help smooth emotional reactions. A heavy night can feel alarming when viewed alone, but less so when placed in a broader context. Conversely, small daily amounts may feel harmless until seen cumulatively.

Why weekly views reduce judgment

Looking at patterns doesn't assign meaning — it simply reveals structure. And structure is easier to understand than isolated events.
