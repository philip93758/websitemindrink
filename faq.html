Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about how Mindrink works, your data, and the limits. For more detailed explanations, see our methodology page or read our blog articles.

Is my drinking history stored in the cloud?

No. All your data is stored locally on your device. We don't sync anything to the cloud automatically. Your privacy is important to us. For more details, see our privacy section.

How accurate are the limits?

The limits are based on public health guidelines, simplified for clarity. They're general recommendations, not personal prescriptions. Everyone's body and situation are different. For a detailed explanation, see our methodology page.

Can I export or delete my data?

Data export is coming soon. You can delete your data at any time (this feature is also coming soon). Uninstalling the app will also clear all your local data. You own your information.

Is Mindrink recommending that I stop drinking?

No. Mindrink is designed for awareness and balance, not abstinence. We believe being fully dry isn't always the answer—awareness, tracking, and small habits can make all the difference. Transparency brings control, and control brings freedom. Learn more about reflecting on your habits.

Does Mindrink work for all types of drinks?

Yes. Mindrink calculates units based on volume and ABV (alcohol by volume), so it works for beer, wine, spirits, cocktails, and any other alcoholic drink. You just need to enter the volume and alcohol percentage. See our guide on what counts as a drink for examples.
