What is an alcohol unit?

An alcohol unit is a standard way to measure the amount of pure alcohol in a drink. In Mindrink, we use 1 unit = 10g of pure alcohol (ethanol). This gives you a consistent measure across all types of drinks, whether it's beer, wine, or spirits.

Limits vs real life

Mindrink shows you guidance levels, but we don't believe in guilt or strict adherence. The limits are tools for awareness, not rules to follow blindly.

Some days you might have more. Some weeks you might have less. That's life. What matters is awarenessâ€”knowing what you're drinking, how it affects you, and making choices that align with your goals.

Our philosophy: guidance, not guilt. Use these numbers to build awareness, not to judge yourself.
